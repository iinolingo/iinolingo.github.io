Baking Sugar Cookies!

Step 1: Preheat Oven to 350 degrees Tip: This part is pretty easy!
Step 2: Cream the butter and sugar together for 4-5 minutes Tip: This just means whipping them together so it can help bring air to the dough
Step 3: Stir in flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt until combined Tip: if you dont have cream of tartar use 1 teaspoon of vinegar or lemon juice for every 1/2 of tartar!
Step 4: In a seperate bowl stir sugar and cinnamon Tip: This step is pretty easy as well!
Step 5: Wrap dough and refrigerate for 20-30 minutes Tip: This is not needed but if you have spare time it would not hurt
Step 6: Drop dough balls in sugar-cinnamon mixture and coat evenly then place on parchment paper into the oven! Tip: You can use a spoon to not get your hands too messy, after taking them out of the oven let them cool for a minute or so!
